About the project

The model adapts Andrew Gelman's 2014 hierarchical Bayesian World Cup model to the 48-team 2026 tournament. Match data comes from the community-maintained martj42/international_results dataset; the prior is the real FIFA/Coca-Cola World Ranking of 3 April 2025. Two models — a Student-t score-difference model and a Dixon-Coles double-Poisson model — are fitted with Stan, and the latter drives a 10,000-run Monte-Carlo simulation of the full knockout bracket.
